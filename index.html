What exactly are NeoPixels?

Neopixels are individually addressable RGB LED's designed with a world of flexibillity and creativity in mind. There are numerous models and revisions to the formula but the most common tools of the trade are the WS2811 LEDS as well as an ESP8266 microcontroller for driving the LEDS (because wireless updates are the best, right?).
